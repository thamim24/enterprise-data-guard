COMPANY SALARY POLICY - VERSION 1.1.1

1. Base Salary Structure:
   - Entry Level: $45,000 - $65,000
   - Mid Level: $70,000 - $85,000
   - Senior Level: $100,000 - $125,000
   - Management: $140,000+

2. Annual Reviews:
   - Performance-based increases: 3-8%
   - Cost of living adjustments: 2-4%

3. Bonus Structure:
   - Individual performance: 5-15% of base
   - Company performance: 2-10% of base
